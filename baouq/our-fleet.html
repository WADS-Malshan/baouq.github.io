    The complete overview at all times

    Are all construction machines where they should be?

    On the map in fleet you can see where your machines are located and in which construction site radius they are moving. The location and allocation is made to construction sites that are already recorded in or can be created directly in fleet.

    Are all construction machines where they should be?

    With 123fleet you benefit from a digital device overview in which you can centrally manage important parameters and dates for your devices. 123fleet shows you the inspection dates and important device information, such as mileage or filling levels, in an easy-to-understand manner. The maintenance measures can be defined individually for the individual devices. You can also upload important documents about inspections and the like directly to the device information in 123fleet.

    Is all construction equipment used optimally?

    With fleet you have an overview of the performance and idle times of all your devices. The machine data is transmitted to fleet by the manufacturer's integrated telematics and by the modules of telematics providers.
