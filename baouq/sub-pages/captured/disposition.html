Good planning is half the battle!

The efficient use of resources and convoy planning are incredibly complex issues in construction. We make it easy for you: Our market-leading app for construction site scheduling is a powerful tool that makes your resource planning easier every day. Resource planning in construction involves many challenges. Most construction sites have many trades that have to carry out different tasks in several shifts. The dispatcher must also take vacation, further training or special regulations on working hours into account when planning the convoy. It's easy to lose track - unless you're using the right software: With 123erckt you can always see at a glance how your employees or equipment are scheduled on the construction site.

Resource planning/scheduling via app - how do I benefit from erckt?

Every shift optimally planned

You can view the planning for each construction site in a clear manner and immediately record which employees are scheduled with which qualifications. In this way you can see whether a construction site is overstaffed or understaffed and take countermeasures.

View schedule anywhere

From home or on the construction site, on the desktop or on the mobile device: your construction workers and employees have access to their shift plan around the clock and can see when and where they are scheduled. Maximum transparency for your colleagues.

Suitable for any size

Regardless of whether you employ 3 or 3000 employees at any number of construction sites , 123erckt offers you all the functions for easily creating and approving your shift plan. For the local hero to the international champion.

Your disposition has never been so easy!

Poor scheduling on the construction site can lead to increased operating costs, overtime, high turnover and increased complaints from employees. Dispatchers or site managers often spend countless hours planning convoys. Overstaffing wastes labor costs, while understaffing leads to wasted time and possible deadline penalties. With 123records you simplify the tedious tasks associated with time tracking and control of your employees.
