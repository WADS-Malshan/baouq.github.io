Photo documentation via app - intuitive & legally compliant

With our market-leading app for photo documentation on construction , your employees on the construction site can use a smartphone to take photos of the current situation, document construction site safety every day and record all the steps in the construction process through to completion with photos. 123erckt enables you to save all services documented on a construction site by means of photos with the GPS coordinates in the app. The images can be assigned directly to the construction site, project, construction phase and construction area. Depending on their authorization, everyone involved in the project has immediate access to the photo documentation and an overview of the progress on your construction site at all times !

What the photo documentation can do for the construction site

We developed 123captured from practice for practice and know how we can make your life easier: With our app for construction site photo documentation, you can not only easily collect photos and data , but also automatically add other relevant information to your construction diary if necessary

Photo documentation on the construction site - what advantages has 123 recorded?

Your customers pay faster

he sooner you bill, the sooner the money will be in your account. A continuous photo documentation with 123erckt supports you. Attach daily construction reports with photos to your invoices. These leave no questions unanswered for your client and are therefore verifiable .

reject complaints

You can also prevent complaints from the client by continuously recording the condition of the construction site with photo documentation. Since the services provided can no longer be checked after the end of the project, thanks to 123erckt the proper implementation is evident and you have increased legal certainty.

Properly log accidents

According to BG regulation A1, accidents, injuries and first-aid services provided must be properly logged. You can fulfill this obligation with a first-aid book stored in 123erfass. In this you take photos of the accident and, if proof is required, you can provide it at the push of a button.

Legally compliant construction site documentation via app

With a complete construction documentation, you can provide your client with detailed photos, weather data, comments and a construction diary of the work carried out in a timely manner, so that discrepancies do not arise in the first place. The construction site photo documentation supports you and provides evidence. Discussions about which service was provided are therefore a thing of the past. And in the event of damage, you are always able to document your proper work to the client, appraiser or the inspection body at the push of a button.
