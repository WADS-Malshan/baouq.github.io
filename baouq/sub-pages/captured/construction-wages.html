Pay slips are a thing of the past!

Payroll accounting for contractors in the construction industry is one of the most complex of all. We make it easy for you: With our app, your personal data goes directly from the construction site to payroll processing - automatically! Post-calculation, invoicing and payroll accounting are simplified and accelerated for you . And you'll never have to hold a payslip in your hand again. Keeping track of labor costs in construction is not easy. With our direct, lossless, completely automatic. market-leading app for construction wages , your colleagues on the construction site can easily record the working times of individual employees or a crew. Our system transfers all data to your payslip.

The benefits for construction

As a development contractor , we know how complex the subject of building wages is and what special features there are in construction. We incorporate this knowledge into our apps to provide you with the best applications!

With 123erckt you can store special construction tariffs, three-month flat rates, different seasonal working hours, seasonal short-time work allowance or winter compensation or your company's own tariff regulations . For example , define company-internal surcharges for overtime, Sunday, public holiday and night work, meal allowances, but also allowances, distance allowances and other expenses and absences . All the peculiarities of the construction wage are taken into account. Who is crying for the payslips?

Construction wage recording for the construction site - how do I benefit from Bauoq recorded?

Always informed on a daily basis

Thanks to the wage data that is filled out daily, you have everything up to date at a glance - from anywhere. In addition, you can also have the data flow directly into the cost centers of the construction sites and see at a glance where your construction site stands economically.

Relieve your payroll department

Your payroll department no longer has to collect handwritten pay slips and manually enter data into the construction payroll software. The effort for controls and incorrect data entry are a thing of the past. It's about time!

Interfaces to many systems

It can't be that easy, can it? But! 123erated adapts to your IT environment. We have over 40 interfaces to the most common payroll programs. With our open interface you automate your processes in the office.
