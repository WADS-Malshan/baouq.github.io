Never again keep a handwritten construction diary!

Digital, mobile, without any effort for you and automatically generated 123records your construction site diary as an app. The software brings together the already recorded construction site data with all comments, services, construction machines used, materials and tools as well as the weather and the employees and subcontractors working there in the construction diary. Your job? Decide whether you would rather have the data as Excel, PDF or printed out! A construction diary not only gives you an overview of the progress of your construction site , but also helps you to prove your performance so that your customers pay faster.It is also often used as evidence in the event of defects or legal disputes. It is all the more important that it is managed cleanly and correctly! Our market-leading construction diary app for iOS (iPad, iPhone), Android and in the browser on the computer automatically generates the digital construction site documentation for your project.

Functions of the 123erckt app for construction

As a development contractor and thanks to our own experience, we know the needs of the construction industry. With our construction diary software 123captured we want to simplify the life of construction companies and support you in keeping your daily construction report efficiently.

With the mobile construction diary app from 123, the data is available to your office in digitized form and in real time - far away from the site. Every authorized employee has access to the construction diary in the 123 office. You can use your smartphone to view the data from anywhere in the world and it's easier than ever before: You can use the software's search function to access entries for specific days or notes.

Create daily construction reports via mobile app - what does 123erckt bring me?

Safe construction supervision

By filling out the construction diary every day, you record what is happening on the construction site and meet your documentation obligations . By documenting the construction process, including all damage and defects, the entries in the construction diary serve as evidence in court in the event of a dispute.

Reliable weather data

The current weather conditions from the German Weather Service are taken over by the software directly on site at any time and assigned to the relevant construction site. This makes it easier for you to argue in the event of delays that may be caused by bad weather

selectively select photos

Already assigned to the right project, you can view all photos in high resolution in the office, add comments and check GPS and time stamps. You decide which photos should also be assigned to the digital construction diary. As a result, the reports for the client are supplemented with the image material .

Save time and money thanks to construction diary software

When keeping the construction diaries by hand, a great deal of effort is required in the event of damage in order to find the corresponding documentation for the respective day. If you also have the handwritten entries digitally recorded, this is time-consuming, costs money and includes a source of error due to the manual transmission of the information. A construction diary app saves both your employees and management time that they can devote to their actual tasks.
