Create your individual forms for the construction site!

Many construction companies give their employees a variety of forms to fill out. Some of these forms for the construction site are essential acceptance reports, checklists or reports of defects. It's all the worse if they don't go back to you at all or are unusable. That's why we offer you the opportunity to easily create your own forms using an app and have them filled out digitally by your employees. By specifying mandatory fields, selecting the master data and validating the data, the forms to be created are clearly designed as soon as you enter them. You can use the form function for: With the 123erckt app, you can easily create your forms from our existing templates in just a few minutes and without any technical knowledge.

Digitally document everything on the construction site

Benefits of digital forms for construction companies

Nobody likes to fill out forms, but unfortunately we can't do without them in construction. As a developer of a construction contractor , we know how to simplify the life of everyone on the construction site with a forms software solution.

If you create your forms and checklists online, you can configure the order and type of entry fields and checkboxes yourself - for example selection fields with fixed selection texts such as "Length", "Height" or "Width", fields for photos and master data such as construction site, Person, device and fields for number and text input , date, traffic light input , QR code and barcode as well as the digital signature . Among other things, it is possible to link the visibility of fields to other fields. If, for example, the "Device damaged" checkbox is ticked, the "Photo of the damage" field to be filled in appears.

Individual forms for the construction site - how do I benefit from recorded?

1a evaluations

All data recorded in the form is transmitted online to the head office and is immediately, completely and permanently available in the form center in the office. Long searches for logs and checklists are a thing of the past!

Set mandatory fields

To make sure that no important entry is forgotten, you can set mandatory fields. Your colleague on the construction site cannot send off the form without filling it out. This saves you annoying and time-consuming inquiries afterwards!

Absolutely flexible

Our customers use the forms in the app for a wide variety of purposes - but above all with a lot of fun! We are always enthusiastic about this ingenuity and, thanks to their flexibility, also use the forms for our processes
