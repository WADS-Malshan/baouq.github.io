Time recording on the construction site with Bauoq recorded: It couldn't be easier!

Put an end to the time sheet at the construction site! With our market-leading app for mobile time recording , your colleagues on the construction site can intuitively record the times of individual employees or an entire crew with a regular smartphone . The system automatically assigns the times to a construction site, the components and construction areas. The mobile recorded times, locations and activities are digitally transferred to a server for documentation. So they look good in your office, e.g. B. for billing and daily construction reports in real time . At the same time, the processed data can be shared online with everyone involved in the project.
